Bio

Zach Comisar a.k.a. Kristi Yang is an American electronic dance music artist. He was born in Staten Island, New York, and at the age of 1 his family moved to Los Angeles where he was then raised. Zach has always been involved with music his entire life and started djing at an early age. Zach’s hobbies include producing and DJing. And at the age of 9 he attended the Scratch DJ Academy where he graduated as the youngest person from the program. Zach's curiosity for producing began as he grew tired of spinning other artists tracks. Since then he has released a slew of edits and remixes on his SoundCloud and a new project titled "Genesis.exe" on all platforms. Yang currently has a single titled, “Nothing More” which has garnered nearly 200,000+ plays on SoundCloud. Yang has been working silently on his debut project which is slated to release this summer thorugh Lee Foss's label Repopulate Mars. Zach is gearing up to emerge as one of the hottest new acts in electronic music.

To go along with his music, Zach has come up with a persona/story to fascinate the listener and viewer of his live prefoprmances. Visulas are frequently released through scoial media and can give the listener a guide to what his music is trying to convey. Based off of the Japanese, manga and anime culture, Zach aims to fascinate his audince with a visual story through manga and animation.
